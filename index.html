stickydesk by gps

A Suite of applications that provide an innovative way of collaborative brainstorming.

This is a project that I worked on for the University of Michigan's Duderstadt Center Design Lab 1. The idea is to allow a group of people to draw on their computers and have the images sent over to a central set of screens for everyone to see. The tablet pc experience is simulated with a WiiMote, IR LED, and Johnny Lee's project, found at: http://johnnylee.net/projects/wii/ For more information, refer the the project's home page at: http://www-personal.umich.edu/~gopalkri/Gopalkrishna_Sharma/Sticky_Desk.html
